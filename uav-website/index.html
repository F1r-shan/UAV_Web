Who We Are

KhachatryanUAV

Established in 2026, KhachatryanUAV is at the forefront of aerospace innovation — specializing in the design and manufacturing of high-performance, long-range FPV fixed-wing UAVs.

Our aircraft use a front-mounted motor configuration for optimal pull-propeller aerodynamics and flight stability. Every design is validated through SolidWorks CFD and FEA simulation — covering drag reduction, lift optimization, thermal management, and structural stress testing before a single part is printed.

Current models run the MatekSYS F405-Wing-V2 flight controller and ExpressLRS (ELRS) radio link — purpose-built for long-range, reliable, open-source operation.
